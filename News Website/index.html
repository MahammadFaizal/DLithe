Political decisions change overnight, says Dy CM

BENGALURU: “Anything can happen in politics overnight,” said president of Karnataka Pradesh Congress Committee (KPCC) and Deputy Chief Minister D.K. Shivakumar in Bengaluru on Monday responding to a query on nominations to the Legislative Council. Shivakumar was asked whether nominations to the Legislative Council can happen before or after the budget session. The budget session will commence on March 3 and Chief Minister Siddaramaiah holding the Finance portfolio is set to present the budget on March 7.
